Stage 5. Correct yellow cross
The resulting cross may be incorrect (Fig. 1). We need to make sure that all the colors on the edges of the cross (3 layers) match the centers (Fig. 2). Attention, we can have only three situations: 1. the edges match next to each other 2. the edges match opposite each other 3. you are lucky, and the cross is correct right away or you just need to turn the top layer.
